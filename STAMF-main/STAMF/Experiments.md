### 1.2. Evaluation Metrics
To comprehensively assess the performance, we employ five standard metrics:
*   **Mean Absolute Error (MAE, $\mathcal{M}$):** Measures the average pixel-wise absolute difference between the predicted saliency map and the ground truth.
*   **F-measure ($F_\beta$):** A weighted harmonic mean of precision and recall. We report both the maximum F-measure ($F_{\beta}^{max}$) and mean F-measure ($F_{\beta}^{mean}$).
*   **S-measure ($S_{\alpha}$):** Evaluates the structural similarity, considering both object-aware and region-aware structural similarity.
*   **E-measure ($E_{\xi}$):** Captures both local pixel matching and global image-level statistics.
*   **Area Under Curve (AUC):** Measures the classification ability of the saliency map at different thresholds.
